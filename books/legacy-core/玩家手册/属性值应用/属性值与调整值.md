## **<span style="FONT-SIZE: 22pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(128,0,0); mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">属性值与调整值</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 22pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(128,0,0); mso-spacerun: 'yes'">Ability Scores and Modifiers</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>**

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">每个生物的各项属性都对应一个数值，反映了该项属性的能力水平。属性值不单作为其天生素质的量化，也涵盖了该生物关于相应属性的后天训练与经验。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">  
</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">普通人类的平均属性值为</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">10</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">至</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">11</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，而冒险者和许多怪物的多项能力都高于常人。一般人的属性值通常最高可以达</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">18</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，而冒险者则可达到</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">20</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，怪物与神甚至可以高至</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">30</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">  
</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">每项属性同时也对应着一项调整值，其数值范围在</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">-5</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">（对应属性值</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">1</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">）到</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">+10</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">（对应属性值</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: Cambria; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">30</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 11pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">）之间。表格“属性值与调整值”列出了可能的属性值范围（1到30）所对应的调整值。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>
