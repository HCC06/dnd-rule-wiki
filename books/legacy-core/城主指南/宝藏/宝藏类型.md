## **<span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">宝藏类型</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">Types of Treasure</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>**

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> 宝藏有许多种形式。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> </span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">钱币</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">Coins</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">。</span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">最基本的宝藏类型就是钱，包括铜币（cp）、银币（sp）、银金币（ep）、金币（gp）和铂金币（pp）。50枚任何类型的硬币都有1磅重。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> </span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">宝石</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">Gemstones</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">。</span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">宝石小巧轻便且比同样价值的硬币更易于保存。下文“宝石”小节中有介绍可用作被发现宝藏的各种奇石、宝石和珠宝。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> </span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">艺术品物件</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">Art Objects</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">。</span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">纯金的神像、镶满宝石的项链、古代国王的绘画、散发着珠光宝气的盘碟等艺术品。下文“艺术品物件”中有介绍可用作被发现宝藏的各种有价值和装饰性的艺术品。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> </span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">魔法物品</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">Magic Items</span>**<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">。</span>***<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">魔法物品类型包括护甲、药水、卷轴、戒指、权杖、法杖、魔杖、武器和奇物。魔法物品还设定了稀有度：普通、非普通、珍稀，极珍稀和传说。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">  
</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> 经常会有一些持有并使用魔法物品的智能怪物，而其他的怪物则会将这些物品藏起来一面弄丢或被盗。比如说，如果一个大地精部族宝库里库存这一把</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">+1长剑longsword</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">和</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">炼金壶alchemy jug</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">，该部族的军阀可能会持用这把宝剑，而壶则会被安放在安全的地方。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>
