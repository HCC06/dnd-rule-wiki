<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"> 你的玩家可能会对本章或</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-ansi-font-style: italic">《玩家手册》</span>*<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">中没提到的休整期活动感兴趣。如果你创作出新的休整活动，请谨记以下几点：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>

<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-ascii-font-family: Symbol; mso-hansi-font-family: Symbol;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore">· </span></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'">活动的金钱花费让玩家有机会花费自己得来不易的财富。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-spacerun: 'yes'"></span>
