药剂酿造（暂缺）

Potion Brewing（翻译暂缺）

Magic abounds on Obojima. It permeates the landscape, and it pools in certain objects. Over the generations, sages discovered that this latent magic can be unleashed through what has become the honored craft of potion brewing. The craft is mysterious, wondrous, and more than a bit chaotic. Most people don't even attempt it. Seasoned brewers can't be entirely certain about what kind of potion will result when combining ingredients. Even so, the tradition remains among the island's proudest.

This chapter discusses how to gather ingredients, how to brew potions, and what kind of potions one can make.
